Ride sharing for the enterprise

Rinity helps co-workers create a ride sharing community to make the daily commuting more affordable and convenient.

Easy

Using our mobile application it is a matter of taps to organize your daily commute. You set the time of arrival and a driver working in the same shift and site will take you to work!

Cheap

It costs you nil. Nada. None. Null. Even public transport can't beat our prices! But if you apply to become a driver, you can get donations from the community.

Comfortable

Ever got cold waiting for the bus or tram? Ever experienced rain at the busstup? Ever annoyed by the people on public transport? No more! With Rinity you can enjoy the compfort of a car and the company of a co-worker!
